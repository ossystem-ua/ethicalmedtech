Welcome

 

We are delighted to invite you to the 9th Advanced Symposium on Congenital Heart Disease in the Adult. Our biennial meeting is held at it’s London home in 2014,  namely at the Royal College of Surgeons of England in the heart of historic London.

 

Although the meeting has gained increased support over the years, the primary objective remains constant: to provide a forum for lively debate on the latest clinical, surgical and scientific developments in the world of adult congenital cardiology. In developing the programme, we hope to have achieved this aim and we would like to encourage everyone attending to make the most of the discussion time included to bring difficult cases to the fore and focus on issues central to their own practice.

 

We encourage you to join us in London for what promises to be a most stimulating and enjoyable event in the 2014 calendar.
